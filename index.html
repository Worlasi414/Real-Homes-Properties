OUR SERVICES

We at Real Homes Properties offers a wide range of fully integrated real estate services through both commercial and residential properties to a diverse clientele.

Property Sales

We offer industry leading property marketing and acquisition services because we know buying or selling property can be a complicated and overwhelming process. We ensure that we thoroughly educate our clients prior selling or buying property on their behalf.

Decorating

We undertake full property redecoration and we go through all the options with our clients. We can tackle from the smallest of painting jobs, right through to special paint effects, marbling and distress detailing using fine paints, stencils and gold leaf.

Interior Design

From traditional sympathetic interior design, taking in consideration of the age and style of the property right through to a contemporary look and anything in-between often combining the two.

Refurbishment

A number of skilled trades exist on and we can with various craftsman undertake to refurbish your new property the way you want without any stress or inconvenience to you.

Project Management

We can oversee the works that need to be carried out, liaising with builders, carpenters, plumbers, electricians, stone masons and so on. Ensuring that the work is done properly, as requested within the timescale and in budget.

Property Management

We can undertake to manage your property when you are away off the island. General repairs, periodic gardening and property checks during bad weather. Regular maintenance work and airing with an inspection report at the end.

Rental

Many people choose to rent out their property whilst they are not enjoying it. We can handle everything there needs to be done to ensure your clients are looked after. From meet and greet from the port, taking them to the property and showing them round to preparing a welcome pack, ensuring the place has been cleaned and ready for the new incoming.
